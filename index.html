About

GoTakeMe is a simple, open-source URL shortener written in Go.
It does not collect any personal information. If you attempt to create or delete a short link, a hash of your IP address will be stored in memory for 1 minute, for the purpose of rate limiting.
You may not attempt to create / delete more than 5 short links per minute. This site does not use any cookies, analytics, or tracking scripts. As no personal information is collected, we can't share any data with third parties.
All link data is indexed by and encrypted with the hash of the short link. This means, that even the site administrator can not know the redirect targets, without knowing the short link.
